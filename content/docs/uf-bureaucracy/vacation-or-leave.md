## Staff and Postdocs
* General Information about Sick Leave, Breaks, Vacation and other work hours policies can be found on the department website under [WEC Leave Policy](https://wec.ifas.ufl.edu/resources/)
* tldr: you can apply for leave in one of two ways:
*    use the [departmental form](https://wec.ifas.ufl.edu/media/wecifasufledu/files/leaveapplication.pdf). Fill it out and send to your supervisor(Ethan or Morgan) for a signature. Then send signed form to WECLeave@ifas.ufl.edu
OR
* Email Ethan or Morgan (whoever your official supervisor is) requesting leave, with the dates, total hours of leave, and type of leave (sick leave, vacation, family emergency?) and put CC @IF-SVC-WECLeave in the email text
  * Ethan or Morgan will reply-all with "Approved" and it will be taken care of.
