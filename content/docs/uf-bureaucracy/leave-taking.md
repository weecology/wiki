Leave documentation can be found on the [WEC Resources page](https://wec.ifas.ufl.edu/resources/).
This includes the [WEC Leave Policy](https://wec.ifas.ufl.edu/media/wecifasufledu/files/personnel/Leave-Policy-09.2021.pdf) and 
[Application for Leave form](https://wec.ifas.ufl.edu/media/wecifasufledu/files/leaveapplication.pdf).

### How to request Leave

* At least 1 week before your leave starts, fill out the ‘Application for Leave’ form.
* Collect your supervisor's signature on the form.
* Email the form to @IF-SVC-WECLeave, cc supervisor.
* This will serve as the official request and approval, so you're done!

Should this form become unavailable, you can also just email WECLeave to start the process:

* At least 1 week before your leave starts, CC @IF-SVC-WECLeave on an email sent to your supervisor requesting leave, including dates, 
total hours, and type of leave.
* Your supervisor should reply-all (e.g., ‘Approved’). This serves as their signature.
* @IF-SVC-WECLeave will confirm the request.
